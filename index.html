About Us

The Southeast Cultural Society (SCS) was founded in 2010 to promote cultural awareness, creativity, and artistic expression among students. We organize cultural festivals, art exhibitions, workshops, and competitions that bring together talent from across the region.

Our vision is to build a community that celebrates diversity and creativity through art and culture.
